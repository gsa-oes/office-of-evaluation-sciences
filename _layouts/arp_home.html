Interagency pandemic relief and recovery: A look at 32 American Rescue Plan programs

The ARP National Evaluation provides information about the 32 programs that were included in the 2022 White House Advancing Equity Through the ARP report.

This work is being conducted by the American Institutes for Research with Abt Associates and Decision Information Resources. It is overseen through a partnership between the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Evidence Team and the General Services Administration (GSA) Office of Evaluation Sciences (OES).
